# Example Queries for Query Optimizer RAG

These are example questions that showcase the system's ability to decompose
complex questions and synthesize comprehensive answers.

## Example 1: Comparative Analysis
Query: "What's the difference between photosynthesis and chemosynthesis, and why did photosynthesis become dominant on Earth?"

This question has multiple implicit parts:
- What is photosynthesis?
- What is chemosynthesis?
- How do they differ?
- Why did photosynthesis become more dominant?

The system decomposes this, retrieves relevant information for each part, then synthesizes a comprehensive answer.

## Example 2: Historical and Functional
Query: "How did the evolution of photosynthetic organisms change Earth's atmosphere, and what were the ecological consequences?"

Sub-questions:
- What was Earth's atmosphere before photosynthesis?
- How did photosynthetic organisms change the atmosphere?
- What were the consequences of these changes?
- How did this enable new life forms?

## Example 3: Multi-step Reasoning
Query: "Explain why chemosynthetic organisms exist today despite photosynthesis being more efficient, and what environments they inhabit."

Sub-questions:
- Why is photosynthesis more efficient than chemosynthesis?
- Why do chemosynthetic organisms still exist?
- Where are they found?
- What are the conditions in those environments?

---

To run these queries:

1. First, index the documents:
   python src/indexing.py

2. Run a query:
   python src/query.py --query "Your question here"

3. For detailed output with intermediate steps:
   python src/query.py --query "Your question here" --verbose
